pricing factors

Good factors to use to determine ur price:


- Set ur price to be similar to ur competitions prices

- Set ur price low if ur customer base is still unestablished 

- Set ur price low if ur customers needs a discount



Bad factors to use to determine ur price: 


- Do not set ur price high if u just spent money on ur fixed assets and feel u need to  get a 100% return instantly		

- Do not up ur price if ur customer wants a product that u dont agree is worth investing in, remember other customers might share their desire
