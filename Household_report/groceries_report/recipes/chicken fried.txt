indgredients

*3 chicken breast
*120ml cooking oil
*1 tablespoon spice (chicken herb and spice)
*1/3 cup flour
*1/2 teaspoon salt

instructs:

1. fillet chicken breast 
2. cut eat breat into 3 slices
3. mix salt spice and flour on a plate
4. batter the chicken
5. preheat oil in pan for 5 min
6. cook 2 pieces at a time on medium heat for 10min turning once
